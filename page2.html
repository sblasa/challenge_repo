Sometimes, it's tough to get to your "Happy Place". What's a "Happy Place"? Well, it's a euphemism to allow yourself to not worry about the things that are mentally bothering you. It can really be anything or even anyone. It's a place you mentally go to calm your mind.

Here are a few things that get me to my happy place:

  • Hot Fudge Brownie Sundae
  • Flowers--yeah, I know. Girly.
  • Mochas
  • Shoes
  • Books
  • Spin Class

    • I wish I could visit these places more often!
